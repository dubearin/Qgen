How to Use QGen: A Step-by-Step Guide

Master the art of question paper generation with these easy steps.

1. Log In to Your Account

Access the QGen platform by logging in with your registered username and password. If you don't have an account, please contact support for registration.

2. Navigate to the Generator

After successful login, you will be directed to the Question Paper Generator interface. If you are on the home page, simply click "Go to Generator" from the hero section or use the corresponding button in the navbar (if logged in).

3. Enter General Exam Details

Fill in the required information for your examination:

  1. Institution Name: Enter the full name of your school, college, or institute.
  2. Exam Name: Specify the name of the exam (e.g., "Mid-Term Exam," "Final Assessment," "Unit Test 3").
  3. Exam Date: Select a future date for the examination using the date picker.
  4. Exam Time: Set the scheduled time for the examination.

4. Choose Your Question Bank Type

QGen offers two types of question banks:

  • Predefined: Select this option to use our extensive library of questions.
    1. Choose your **Branch** (e.g., "Computer Science Engineering," "1st Year").
    2. Select the relevant **Subject** from the dropdown list that appears after branch selection.
  • Custom: Select this option if you wish to use your own set of questions.
    1. Enter a **Custom Subject Name** for your paper.
    2. **Paste Your Custom Questions:** Type or paste your questions directly into the text area. Ensure each question is on a new line.
    3. **Upload a Plain Text (.txt) File:** Alternatively, you can upload a `.txt` file containing your questions, with each question on a new line.
    4. Click the "Load Custom Questions" button to process your input.

5. Select Total Marks

Indicate the total marks for the paper by choosing either "20 Marks" or "80 Marks". The system will automatically adjust the number and type of questions accordingly.

6. Generate and Preview

Once all details are filled and questions are loaded (either predefined or custom), click the "Generate Question Paper" button. The system will process your request and open a new window with the generated question paper in a printable format.

7. Print or Download

In the new window, you will see buttons to "Print" the paper directly or "Back to Generator" to close the window and return to the main application.

8. Return to Home

From the generator page, you can click the "Back to Home" button to return to the main QGen homepage.
